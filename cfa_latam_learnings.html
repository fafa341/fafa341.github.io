CFA LATAM Report: Learnings

Comprehensive Analysis of Latin American Markets

Key Learnings

Teamwork was key in this. Aligning responsibilities and deadlines was critical.

For finance usually techniques for evaluating companies that have been working for the past 30 years work well now.

For tool usage the simpler the better. Linear regression does wonders.

Deep research tool with Gemini is key to get access to deeper insights + intuition and field knowledge. That's the combo: uncover how the macro works and tie it to the micro.

Exceptional experience to learn how to tie qualitative aspects move quantitative details and vice versa.

Exceptional risk analysis is achieved when context about company history, industry and competition is fully gathered and understood.

Trying to predict the finance market is not reliable with no sufficient information.

Market Insights

It's interesting to see how loyalty programs are a priority for most companies. Delivering a unique experience has never been that important. Must understand their users for this. Really well.

Challenges and Solutions

Gathering data was quite hard not because of insufficient information but irrelevant information. Learned how to detect truly useful information.

Had a good experience with Bloomberg terminal. Learning curve is not steep. Quite simple actually. I can see how to leverage this especially with specific automation pipelines if possible.

Power of synthesis and communication. Every technical or major discovery is useless unless we can communicate its impact. Learned how to communicate effectively.
